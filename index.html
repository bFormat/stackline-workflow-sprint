How to play

Pieces fall from the top — rotate and shift them so that complete rows clear. Gravity speeds up as you level up.

Keyboard:

←/→ move · ↓ soft drop · Space hard drop · ↑/X rotate CW · Z rotate CCW · Shift/C hold · P/Esc pause · R restart

Touch: on-screen buttons, or swipe on the playfield.
